Building India's
Circular Economy

Reloop is more than a marketplace—it's a movement to transform how we think about waste, value local craftsmanship, and build sustainable communities.

Our Mission

Every day, millions of items end up in landfills—items that still have value, beauty, and potential. Meanwhile, talented artisans struggle to find affordable materials and reach customers who appreciate their craft.

Reloop bridges this gap. We've created a hyper-local ecosystem where:

  • Givers can easily list discardable materials, giving them a second life instead of sending them to landfills.
  • Makers get access to free or low-cost materials within their neighborhood, reducing their costs and environmental footprint.
  • Buyers discover unique, handcrafted products with a story, supporting local artisans and sustainable practices.

Our Values

Community First

We believe in building strong, supportive local communities where everyone thrives together.

Sustainability

Every item diverted from landfills is a step towards a greener, more sustainable future.

Empowerment

Circular Economy

We're building a closed-loop system where waste becomes a valuable resource.

Why We Started Reloop

The idea for Reloop was born from a simple observation: in Indian cities, we have both a waste problem and an opportunity problem.

Our founders noticed their neighbor throwing away perfectly good wooden furniture while a talented carpenter struggled to afford raw materials. That disconnect inspired the vision for Reloop—a platform that turns "waste" into opportunity.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of users across Bengaluru, and we're just getting started. Our vision is to expand across India, making the circular economy accessible to everyone.
